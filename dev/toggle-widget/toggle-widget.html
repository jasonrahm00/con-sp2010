The Belleview Point Clinic is comprised of Nurse Practitioners and Licenced Behavioral Health providers who focus on whole person care. The Belleview Point Clinic Providers work together by attending to all things that may be impacting the health of a patient from head to toe. Nurse Practitioners work with patients to help them find and pursue good options for healthy living by incorporating health promotion, disease prevention, and health education and counseling.

Our Vision

Improve the healthcare experience in an integrated model of care that decreases associated healthcare costs and delivers improved health outcomes in the communities that we serve.

Our Mission

Delivering high quality care and customer service in a nurturing and pleasant environment which encourages partnerships amongst providers, staff and patients.

Our Values
  • Inspire the pursuit of healthy living.
  • Foster mutual respect and compassion.
  • Promote an accessible and cohesive work environment.
  • Commit to continuous improvement.
  • Cultivate a culture of integrity, dependability & continuity.
  • Contribute to wellbeing of our community through regular community involvement.
  • Maintain a responsibility to explore new opportunities and innovations that support the vision and mission of the clinic​​ and the CU College of Nursing.

Appointments

We offer same or next day appointments for most common ailments and services. Please call or walk-in and we will do our best to see you ASAP.

Insurance and Cash Payment Options

We accept Anthem, Cigna, United, Rocky Mountain, Colorado Access, Medicare, BHI, Anthem Behavioral Health, CU Exclusive Network, and Medicaid, or patients can pay by cash. Patients requiring on-going primary care can also join our direct primary care membership program (not available to those on government-funded health insurance).

View Our Refund Policy
Refund Policy
  • All requests for refunds must be routed to the operations manager for approval.
  • The operations manager will respond to requests for refunds within 48 business hours of the request.
  • If it is determined that a refund is warranted, the operations manager will notify the front desk staff.
  • The operations manager and front desk staff will work together to process the refund.
  • All refunds will be processed against original form of payment. If a credit card was the form of payment, a refund will be processed against the original credit card used for payment.
